FAQ

Who can participate?

Any college-going student with a valid ID card. Engineering, Arts, doesn't matter!

How much does it cost?

Rs. 300 per person. We'll take care of all your meals & refreshments! You'll pay this only if you're selected for the on-site round. Online round is free of cost!

What should I bring?

Your college ID, your phone, laptop, chargers and any electronics you need. A change of clothes is also recommended along with sleeping equipment that you might want.

What shouldn't I bring?

Drugs, alcohol, weapons of any kind. If you're unsure of it, ask us domains it.

What do you get?

You get to meet new people with similar interests, compete to win a massive prize, have opportunities to gain internships or jobs from some cool companies, an amazing learning experience and some awesome memories. :)

How big can my team be?

You can have a team of 2-3 members from the same or from different institutions. We recommend having a team with a mix of design and development skills.
